Abbreviations test

Sometimes we need to display abbreviated terms on a website but have the screen-reader read aloud the full word. Scenarios might be when a time is shortened to 2hr 30mins or 2h 30m to mean 2 hours 30 minutes. VoiceOver (Apple OSs) correctly cguess that we mean 'hours' and 'miunutes' but JAWS (Windows) does not.

The options are abbr tags or aria-label attributes.

abbr tag with a title attribute

  • 2hr 30mins
  • 2hr 30min
  • 2h 30m

aria-label on a span tag

  • 2hr 30mins
  • 2hr 30 mins
  • 2hr 30min
  • 2h 30m

time tag

  • 2h 30m
